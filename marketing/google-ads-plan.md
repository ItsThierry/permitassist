## Conversion Tracking
Set up Google Ads conversion tracking for:
1. **Signup** — when user enters email (email gate)
2. **Paid conversion** — Stripe checkout redirect
3. **Lookup completed** — when a permit result is shown

## Expected Performance (Month 1)
- Budget: $200
- Est. CPC: $1.50-$3.00 (permit keywords are niche/low competition)
- Est. clicks: 65-130/month
- Est. trial rate: 5-8%
- Est. new trials: 3-10/month
- Est. paid conversion: 15-25% of trials
- Est. new paying users: 1-3/month

## Scaling Plan
- Month 1: $200, validate which ad groups convert
- Month 2: Pause non-performing, shift budget to winners
- Month 3: If ROAS > 2x, consider increasing to $400/month
- Target: Break even at ~11 paying users ($19 × 11 = $209)
