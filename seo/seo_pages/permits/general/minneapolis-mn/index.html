🔍 What Requires a General Contractor Permit in Minneapolis?

In Minneapolis, Minnesota, a building permit is typically required for:

  • New construction or additions
  • Interior remodels affecting structure or systems
  • Garage conversions or ADU construction
  • Deck or porch additions over 200 sq ft
  • Commercial tenant improvements
⚠️ Always verify — permit requirements vary by project scope, zoning, and property type. When in doubt, call Minneapolis Building Department at (612) 673-3000.
