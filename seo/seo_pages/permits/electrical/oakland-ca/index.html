🔍 What Requires a Electrical Permit in Oakland?

In Oakland, California, a electrical permit is typically required for:

  • Electrical panel upgrades or replacements
  • New circuit installation
  • Adding outlets or rewiring rooms
  • Service entrance work
  • EV charger installation
⚠️ Always verify — permit requirements vary by project scope, zoning, and property type. When in doubt, call Oakland Building Department at 510-238-3891.
