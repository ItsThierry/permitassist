Questions?

Can I try it before paying? ▼
Yes — every account gets 3 free lookups per month, no credit card required. Just do a lookup on the homepage and we'll create your account automatically.
How do I cancel? ▼
Go to your Account page and click "Manage Billing." You can cancel anytime — your access continues until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) through Stripe. Your card info never touches our servers.
What if my city isn't covered? ▼
We still research your permit requirements in real-time using AI, pulling from official building department sources. Pro and Team users can also request specific cities to be added to our verified database — we add them within a week.
Is the data accurate? ▼
Our verified city data is cross-checked against official building department sources. Every result shows its confidence level and sources. We always recommend confirming final details with the local building department before submitting — permit rules change, and we want you to be 100% sure.
Can my office manager handle billing? ▼
Yes — on the Team plan, any team member with account access can manage billing through the billing portal. Invite your office manager via the Account page.
