PermitAssist Help

How PermitAssist works, what the confidence labels mean, and how to get the most reliable result.

1. Best way to search

  • Add the full job type, not just the trade. Example: “200 amp panel upgrade” instead of “electrical work”.
  • Add the job address when you have it, especially for county and unincorporated jobs.
  • Use the residential/commercial toggle correctly.

2. Confidence labels

  • Verified Data: matched against curated city/trade data.
  • AI-Researched: researched live from current web sources.
  • County Data: exact city data was limited, county fallback used.
  • General Estimate: limited local data, confirm directly with the city.

3. What to verify before submitting

  • Permit type
  • Fee amount
  • Who can pull the permit
  • Inspection steps
  • Portal or application link

4. Saved jobs and reminders

Saved jobs are tied to your account. If you set an expiry date, PermitAssist can remind you before the permit expires.

5. Need to share or send a report?

Use Share, Email to Me, Print / PDF, or Download Report from the result screen.

6. Important note

PermitAssist is built to save time and reduce mistakes, but permit rules change often. Always verify final requirements directly with the local building department before starting work.
